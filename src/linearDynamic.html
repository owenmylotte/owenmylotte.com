Linear Dynamic Systems

An understanding of linear dynamics is fundamental. Linear dynamic systems are used to model solid, fluid, thermal, and electrical systems. Therefore, the concepts embedded in these second order differential equations are Through these concepts, we can build a mathematical framework to model and predict the behavior of systems to any conditions, including time-varying loads, vibrations, impulses, and more. Linear dynamics assumes that the motion of systems can be modeled by linear differential equations, meaning that the time derivatives of position are proportional to the applied force.
